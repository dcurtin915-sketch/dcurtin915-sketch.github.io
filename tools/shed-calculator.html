How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your shed length and width (footprint dimensions).
  2. Set the wall height — 8 ft is standard for sheds.
  3. Choose a roof pitch — 4/12 is the most common for sheds.
  4. Select stud spacing (16" OC is standard, 24" OC for lighter builds).
  5. Click "Calculate Materials" for your complete material list.

The Formula

Wall Studs = (Perimeter ÷ Spacing) + 1 per wall + corner studs
Plates = Perimeter × 3 (double top plate + bottom plate)
Roof Area = Length × (Width ÷ 2 × Pitch Factor) × 2 sides
Shingle Bundles = Roof Area ÷ 33.3 sq ft/bundle

The roof pitch factor converts the horizontal run into actual rafter length. Floor joists are spaced at 16" OC. All quantities include a 10% waste factor.
