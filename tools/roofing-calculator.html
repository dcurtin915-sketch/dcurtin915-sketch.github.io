How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the length and width of the roof footprint (the building footprint).
  2. Enter the roof pitch as rise over run (e.g., 6:12 means 6 inches of rise per 12 inches of run).
  3. Set a waste factor (10% is typical for standard roofs, 15% for complex roofs).
  4. Click "Calculate Roofing" for a complete materials list.

The Formula

Pitch Multiplier = √(rise² + run²) ÷ run
Roof Area = Footprint × Pitch Multiplier
Squares = (Roof Area × (1 + Waste%)) ÷ 100

One roofing square = 100 sq ft. Standard 3-tab shingles require 3 bundles per square. Underlayment rolls cover approximately 4 squares each. Ridge cap and nails are estimated proportionally.
