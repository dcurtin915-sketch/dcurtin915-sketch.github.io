How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select your fence type — wood or chain link.
  2. Enter the total length of your fence run in feet.
  3. Set the fence height and post spacing (6 or 8 feet is standard).
  4. For wood fences, enter the picket width (standard is 5.5 inches).
  5. Click "Calculate Fence Materials" to get your complete material list.

The Formula

For wood fences:

Posts = (Total Length ÷ Post Spacing) + 1 + Gates
Rails = Sections × Rails per Section (2 for ≤6ft, 3 for taller)
Pickets = (Total Length × 12) ÷ (Picket Width + Gap)

For chain link:

Fabric = Total Length in linear feet
Top Rail Sections = Length ÷ 10.5 ft

Concrete is estimated at 1 bag (50 lb) per post for fences up to 6 ft, and 2 bags per post for taller fences.
