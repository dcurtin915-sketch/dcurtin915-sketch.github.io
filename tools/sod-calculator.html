How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the length and width of your lawn area in feet.
  2. Select the sod roll size your supplier offers.
  3. Set a waste factor (5–10% is typical for rectangular lawns; use 10–15% for irregular shapes).
  4. Optionally enter a price per roll for a cost estimate.
  5. Click "Calculate Sod" to see your results.

The Formula

Lawn Area = Length × Width
Total Coverage = Lawn Area × (1 + Waste%)
Rolls Needed = ⌈Total Coverage ÷ Roll Size⌉
Pallets = ⌈Rolls ÷ Rolls per Pallet⌉

Standard sod rolls are 2 ft × 5 ft (10 sq ft). A typical pallet holds 50 rolls covering 500 sq ft. Prices vary by grass type and region — confirm with your sod supplier.
