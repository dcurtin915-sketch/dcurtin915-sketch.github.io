How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the room length and width in feet.
  2. Enter the ceiling height (default is 8 feet).
  3. Enter the number of doors and windows to deduct.
  4. Click "Estimate Drywall" to see sheets, tape, compound, and screws needed.

Standard deductions: doors = 21 sq ft (3×7), windows = 15 sq ft (3×5). The calculator includes ceiling coverage.

The Formula

Wall Area = 2 × (Length + Width) × Height
Ceiling Area = Length × Width
Net Area = Wall Area − (Doors × 21) − (Windows × 15) + Ceiling
Sheets = Net Area ÷ 32 (rounded up)

Each 4×8 sheet covers 32 square feet. Tape, compound, and screws are estimated proportionally based on total area.
