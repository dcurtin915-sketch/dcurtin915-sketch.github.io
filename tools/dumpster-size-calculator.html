How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select your project type from the dropdown.
  2. Enter the project area in square feet (room size, roof area, or yard area).
  3. Choose the debris type — heavy debris needs a smaller dumpster due to weight limits.
  4. Click "Calculate Dumpster Size" for recommendations.
  5. When in doubt, size up — it's cheaper than a second haul.

The Formula

Estimated Volume = Project Area × Debris Factor (cu yd per sq ft)

Debris factors vary by project type:

  • Home cleanout: ~0.01 cu yd/sq ft (1 yd per 100 sq ft of home)
  • Roofing: ~0.01–0.015 cu yd/sq ft (1 yard per ~33 sq of roofing)
  • Remodel: ~0.015–0.025 cu yd/sq ft depending on scope
  • Demolition: ~0.025–0.05 cu yd/sq ft
Standard sizes: 10, 15, 20, 30, 40 cubic yards

Heavy materials (concrete, dirt) usually max out at 10–15 yard dumpsters due to weight limits (typically 2–4 tons for 10-yard, 4–6 tons for 20-yard).
