How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select your conduit type (EMT, PVC, RMC) and trade size.
  2. Choose wire size, insulation type, and quantity.
  3. Click "Add Wire Group" to add conductors (repeat for mixed sizes).
  4. Click "Calculate Fill" to check NEC compliance.

The Formula

NEC Table 1 Fill Limits:
1 wire: 53% of conduit area
2 wires: 31% of conduit area
3+ wires: 40% of conduit area

Fill % = (Total Wire Cross-Section Area ÷ Conduit Internal Area) × 100
Wire areas from NEC Chapter 9, Table 5

Conduit internal areas and wire cross-section areas are from NEC Chapter 9, Tables 4 and 5. These are the same tables electricians use for permit compliance.
