How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the number of people and bathrooms in your home.
  2. Enter how many fixtures run during your busiest hour (e.g., morning rush).
  3. Set your inlet water temperature (50°F is typical; colder in northern states).
  4. Click "Calculate Water Heater Size" for tank and tankless recommendations.

The Formula

Peak Hour Demand (gallons):
Shower = 20 gal × count
Bathtub = 36 gal × count
Dishwasher = 14 gal × count
Clothes Washer = 30 gal × count
Hand/Kitchen = 4 gal × count

First Hour Rating (FHR) = Peak Demand (match to tank label)
Tankless GPM = Total GPM of simultaneous fixtures
Tankless BTU = GPM × (Output Temp − Inlet Temp) × 500
Output Temp = 120°F (standard)

Peak hour demand values are from DOE/ENERGY STAR guidelines. The First Hour Rating (FHR) on the tank's EnergyGuide label should meet or exceed your peak demand.
