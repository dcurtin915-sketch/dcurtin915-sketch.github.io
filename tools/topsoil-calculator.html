How to Use This Calculator

  1. Choose your input method — enter length × width or total square feet.
  2. Select the desired depth of topsoil (4" is standard for new garden beds).
  3. Optionally enter a price per cubic yard for cost estimates.
  4. Click "Calculate Topsoil" to see volume and weight results.
  5. Tip: Order 10% extra to account for settling and compaction.

The Formula

Cubic Yards = (Area in sq ft × Depth in inches) ÷ 324

This divides by 12 (inches to feet) and by 27 (cubic feet to cubic yards). Combined: 12 × 27 = 324.

Weight (tons) = Cubic Yards × 1.1

Topsoil weighs approximately 2,200 lbs (1.1 tons) per cubic yard, depending on moisture content.
