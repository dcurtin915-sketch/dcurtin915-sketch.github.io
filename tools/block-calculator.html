How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the wall length and height in feet.
  2. Select the block size — standard 8×8×16 is most common for structural walls.
  3. Choose your mortar joint thickness (⅜" is standard).
  4. Click "Calculate Blocks" for a complete material list.
  5. Results include 5% waste factor and rebar estimates for every 4 feet.

The Formula

For standard 8×8×16 blocks with ⅜" mortar joints:

Blocks per sq ft = 1 ÷ ((Block Height + Joint) × (Block Width + Joint)) in sq ft
Standard: ~1.125 blocks per sq ft of wall face

Mortar: approximately 3.5 bags (70 lb) per 100 blocks.

Rebar: vertical bars every 4 feet plus horizontal reinforcement every 4 feet of height.
