How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your deck length and width in feet.
  2. Select the board width (5.5" is standard for most decking).
  3. Choose the board length available at your supplier.
  4. Set the gap spacing between boards (⅛" is standard for wood).
  5. Select a waste factor — 10% is recommended for most builds, 15% for diagonal or complex layouts.
  6. Click "Calculate Deck Materials" for your full material list.

The Formula

Boards Across = (Deck Width × 12) ÷ (Board Width + Gap)
Boards Along Length = Deck Length ÷ Board Length
Total Boards = Boards Across × Boards Along Length × (1 + Waste%)

Joists are calculated at 16" on center: (Deck Length × 12 ÷ 16) + 1.

Deck screws are estimated at ~350 per 100 sq ft (2 screws per board per joist crossing).
