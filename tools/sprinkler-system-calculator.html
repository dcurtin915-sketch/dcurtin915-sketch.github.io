How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your total lawn area in square feet.
  2. Select your sprinkler head type — rotors for large areas, sprays for small/narrow areas.
  3. Enter your available GPM (do a bucket test: fill a 5-gal bucket and time it).
  4. Enter water pressure in PSI (check with a pressure gauge on your hose bib).
  5. Click "Calculate" for head counts, zones, and pipe estimates.

The Formulas

Heads = Lawn Area ÷ Coverage per Head
Heads per Zone = Available GPM ÷ GPM per Head
Zones = Total Heads ÷ Heads per Zone

Typical Values

  • Rotor heads: ~1,200 sq ft coverage, 2–4 GPM each (30' radius head-to-head)
  • Spray heads: ~200 sq ft coverage, 1–2 GPM each (12' radius head-to-head)
  • Drip: ~1 sq ft per emitter at 12" spacing, 0.5–1 GPH each
