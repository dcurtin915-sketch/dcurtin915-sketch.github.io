How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your room length and width in feet.
  2. Choose the wainscoting height — 36 inches (3 ft) is the most common.
  3. Enter the panel width (standard beadboard panels are 48 inches wide).
  4. Deduct doorways where wainscoting won't be installed.
  5. Click "Calculate Wainscoting" for your full material list.

The Formula

Perimeter = 2 × (Length + Width)
Net LF = Perimeter − (Doors × Door Width)
Panels = ⌈(Net LF × 12 ÷ Panel Width) × (1 + Waste%)⌉
Chair Rail LF = Net LF × (1 + Waste%)

Standard beadboard panels are 48 inches wide and come in heights from 32" to 48". Chair rail goes on top of the wainscoting. One tube of construction adhesive covers roughly 32 linear feet of panel.
