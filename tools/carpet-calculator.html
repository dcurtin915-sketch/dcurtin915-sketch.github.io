How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the room length and width in feet.
  2. Select the carpet roll width (12 ft is most common).
  3. Set the waste factor — 10% is standard; use 15% for patterned carpet.
  4. Optionally enter carpet and padding cost per square foot.
  5. Click "Calculate Carpet" to see your results.

The Formula

Room Area = Length × Width
Total with Waste = Room Area × (1 + Waste%/100)
Linear Feet of Roll = Total Sq Ft ÷ Roll Width
Square Yards = Total Sq Ft ÷ 9
Padding = Same sq ft as carpet

Carpet is sold by the square yard or square foot, cut from rolls typically 12 ft wide. The calculator rounds up linear feet to avoid short pieces. Always order a bit extra for seams and pattern matching.
