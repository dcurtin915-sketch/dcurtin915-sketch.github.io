How to Use This Calculator

  1. Choose Room Dimensions or Total Square Footage input mode.
  2. Enter measurements, number of coats (2 is standard), and deductions.
  3. Click "Calculate Paint" to see gallons needed.
  4. Standard deductions: doors = 21 sq ft, windows = 15 sq ft.

The Formula

Gallons = (Net Area × Coats) ÷ 350

Standard interior latex paint covers approximately 350 square feet per gallon. Net area = total wall area minus doors (21 sq ft each) and windows (15 sq ft each). Results are rounded up to the nearest gallon.
