How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter room dimensions or the total perimeter in linear feet.
  2. Select the piece length your trim comes in (most common: 8' or 12').
  3. Set a waste factor — 10% is standard for miter cuts and mistakes.
  4. Enter the number of inside and outside corners in the room.
  5. Click "Calculate Crown Molding" for your material list.

The Formula

Perimeter = 2 × (Length + Width)

For rectangular rooms. For irregular rooms, measure each wall and add them up.

Linear Feet Needed = Perimeter × (1 + Waste%/100)
Pieces = ⌈Linear Feet ÷ Piece Length⌉

Always round up to whole pieces. Standard waste factor is 10% to account for miter cuts, coping, and off-cuts.
