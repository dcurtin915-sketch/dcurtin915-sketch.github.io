How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select your project type — wall (bricks on face) or patio (bricks flat).
  2. Enter the length and height (walls) or length and width (patios).
  3. Choose your brick size and mortar joint width (⅜" is standard).
  4. Set a waste factor — 5% for simple projects, 10% for cuts and patterns.
  5. Click "Calculate Bricks" for your count and mortar estimate.

The Formula

Wall: Bricks/sq ft = 144 ÷ ((Brick Length + Joint) × (Brick Height + Joint))
Patio: Bricks/sq ft = 144 ÷ ((Brick Length + Joint) × (Brick Width + Joint))

Standard bricks laid in running bond with ⅜" joints = approximately 6.75 bricks per sq ft for walls (face) and 4.5 bricks per sq ft for patios (flat).

Mortar: ~7 bags (70 lb) per 1,000 bricks for walls
