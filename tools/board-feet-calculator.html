How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the board thickness in inches (e.g., 1 for 4/4 lumber, 2 for 8/4).
  2. Enter the board width in inches and length in feet.
  3. Set the quantity of boards you're ordering.
  4. Optionally enter a price per board foot for a cost estimate.
  5. Click "Calculate Board Feet" for instant results.

The Formula

Board Feet = (Thickness" × Width" × Length') ÷ 12

One board foot equals a piece of wood 1 inch thick, 12 inches wide, and 1 foot long (144 cubic inches). Multiply BF per piece by quantity for total board feet.
